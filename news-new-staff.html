New set of staff introduced
- Department of Teacher's Training and Professional Development
10 April, 2024 at 12:00pm

Subject: Welcome Our New Staff Members!

Dear Parents, Guardians, and Students,

We are delighted to announce the addition of three new staff members to our school community! Their diverse skills and experience bring fresh perspectives and ideas that will enhance the educational experience for our kids.

Let’s take a moment to introduce our new staff:

• OLAYIWOLA, Samson Tobilola
- Class Teacher for Primary 4
Mr Olayiwola brings eight years of experience/unique expertise and a passion for Mathematics. We're excited about the positive impact he'll have on our pupils.

• AGATHA, Ngozi Esther
- School Gardening Instructor
With a background in Plant Biology, Mrs Ngozi will be leading Gardening Practices. We look forward to her innovative approach and dedication to teaching.

• RICHARDSON, Nancy Deborah
- School Spanish Instructor
Mrs Richardson has extensive experience in Spanish Language, and we are thrilled to welcome her to our team. Her knowledge and enthusiasm will greatly benefit our pupils.

These new team members will begin working with us effective from next week Monday. We encourage you to join us in extending a warm welcome to them. They are committed to providing a supportive and enriching learning environment for your children.

If you have any questions or would like to learn more about our new staff, please don't hesitate to reach out to our school office.

Thank you for your continued support, and let's give our new staff members a great start in our school community!

Sincerely,
Mrs. Abosede Adeyinka,
Department of Teacher's Training and Professional Development,
Castle Primary School, Lagos
