Upcoming Excursion to Eiffel Tower, Paris, France
- Directorate of Excursions and Travels
13 April, 2024 at 7:49am

Subject: Excursion to Eiffel Tower - Important Information

Dear Parents and Guardians,

We are excited to announce that our school has planned an excursion to the Eiffel Tower for our students on 30th of August, 2024. This is a fantastic opportunity for students to learn about the iconic monument and experience a rich cultural outing.

Here are the important details for the excursion:
- Date: 30 August, 2024
- Departure Time: 8:00am
- Return Date: 5 September, 2024
- Location: Eiffel Tower, Paris
- Transportation: Muritala Muhammed International Airport, Lagos

Pupils should arrive at school by 7:00am to ensure we depart on schedule. Please make sure your child wears comfortable clothing and shoes, as there will be some walking involved.

Please pack a packed lunch and drinks for your child. We also recommend sending them with a small amount of money if they wish to purchase souvenirs.

Permission slips have been sent home with your child. Please ensure they are signed and returned by 20 April, 2024.

If you have any questions or concerns, please feel free to contact any member of the Management.

We are looking forward to a wonderful and educational day at the Eiffel Tower!

Best regards,
Directorate of Excursions and Travels,
Castle Primary School, Lagos
