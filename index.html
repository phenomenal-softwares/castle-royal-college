• OUR OFFERS

Our school stands out as a top choice for many. Here are some key services we offer

Primary & Secondary School

Primary and secondary education are provided to enhance enquiry-based learning, equipping students with critical thinking, collaboration, problem-solving and global competence skills essential for the 21st century

Nigerian and African Studies

We celebrate our heritage by integrating the study of Nigerian and African languages, landscapes and cultural identity.

Infant care

The best infant caregivers and babysitters are employed to give infants a homely and motherly experience

Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics (STEM)

Through hands-on activities, project-based learning, and exposure to cutting-edge technologies, we equip students with the critical thinking and problem-solving skills needed to thrive in the 21st century.

Qualified Personnels

In terms of child care and impartation of knowledge, our staff - both teaching and non-teaching - are carefully selected after having met necessary requirements

happy and secure environment

In our school, child security and overall well-being is a top priority. A number of measures have been put in place to ensure a secure as well as a conducive atmosphere

Holistic education

In the pursuit of an all-round education, the students also benefit from a comprehensive program of study in Foreign Languages, the Arts, Musical Education and Sporting Activities.
