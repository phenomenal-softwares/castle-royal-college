Deadline For All Tuition payment
- Management
15 April, 2024 at 10:30am

Subject: Payment Deadline Reminder

Dear Parents and Guardians,

We hope this message finds you well. We would like to kindly remind you that the deadline for submitting tuition payments for third term is approaching. The due date for payments is 30 April, 2024.

To avoid any interruptions in your child's education and access to school resources, please make the payment on or before the deadline. If you have already made the payment, please ignore this prompt.

For any questions or concerns regarding your account or payment options, please contact the school finance office at the school premises. Transfers to our school bank account is encouraged.

Thank you for your prompt attention to this matter.

Best regards,
Castle Primary School, Lagos
