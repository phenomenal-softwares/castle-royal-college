Damilola Godwin from Primary 4 wins Student Neatness Award for the week
- Department of Students Welfare
9 April, 2024 at 2:35pm

Subject: Congratulations to Damilola Godwin for Winning the Student Neatness Award!

Dear Parents and Guardians,

We are thrilled to announce that Damilola Godwin has been awarded the Student Neatness Award for this week! This recognition is a testament to Damilola's consistent effort to maintain a tidy, organized, and clean personal and shared space within the school.

Her commitment to upholding high standards of neatness not only sets a great example for her peers but also contributes positively to our learning environment. We believe that a clean and organized setting is conducive to successful learning, and Damilola's diligence helps foster this atmosphere.

Please join us in congratulating Damilola Godwin on this well-deserved achievement! We look forward to seeing more students follow in her footsteps and continue to make our school a cleaner and more organized place for everyone.

It should also be noted that a befitting gift has been sent to her parents as a token of compensation!

Best regards,
Department of Students Welfare,
Castle Primary School, Lagos
